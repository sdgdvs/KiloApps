Help / Quick Reference
Dialing Directory: Save and quick-connect to your favorite BBSs.
XMODEM / ZMODEM: Transfer files using classic protocols. Click DL to download from the BBS, UL to upload.
ANSI Music: Fully supports ANSI-M music codes. Play tones directly from BBS games and menus.
Macros: Bind Alt+Key to string sequences. Use \r for Enter. Great for quick logins.
Display Settings: Adjust font size, blink rate, and the 16-color PC palette.
Scrollback: Use the scrollbar or Shift+PgUp/PgDn to view terminal history.
Copy/Paste: Click and drag to select text, then copy. Ctrl+V to paste into the terminal.
Session Capture: Toggle capture ON to save all incoming text to a local file.
Baud Rate Emulation: Simulate old modem speeds (e.g. 2400 bps) in Settings.
