How to Play KGo

Goal: Control more territory (empty points) than your opponent.

  • Players (Black and White) take turns placing stones on board intersections.
  • Liberties & Capture: Stones must have at least 1 empty adjacent point (liberty). If surrounded, they are captured.
  • Ko Rule: Cannot recreate the exact previous board state.
  • Suicide Rule: Cannot place a stone with 0 liberties unless it captures opponent stones.
  • Scoring: Empty intersections surrounded + captured stones + Komi (White bonus).

Hotkeys & Features:

  • U Key: Undo last move pair (VS AI).
  • H Key: AI Hint - Evaluates and highlights the optimal move.
  • T Key: Territory Estimator - Overlays current territory estimation & flags stones in Atari (yellow border).
