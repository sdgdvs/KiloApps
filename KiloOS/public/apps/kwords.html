    How to Play KWords

    Goal: Find all the hidden words in the grid!

    • Words can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal (forwards or backwards).
    • Click and drag to select a word in the grid.

    Campaign & Modes

    • Campaign Mode: 15 stages with distinct grid sizes, themes, stage timers, and frozen tile obstacles!
    • Classic / Time Attack / Zen: Play custom difficulties.

    Power-Ups & Mechanics

    • Radar / Reveal Word (R key): Highlights an unfound word completely.
    • Freeze Timer (F key): Freezes countdown timer for 15 seconds!
    • Hint / First Letter (H key): Flashes starting letter of an unfound word.
    • Frozen Tiles: Locked cyan tiles on the grid that thaw when finding words touching them!
