General Controls

Aim with mouse and click to throw. Aim wobbles dynamically over time. Throw 3 darts per turn.

Power-Ups & Shortcuts

  • Focus (F): Slows crosshair wobble speed by 50%. (2 uses)
  • Magnet (M): Expands hit tolerance for Double and Triple rings. (2 uses)
  • Undo Dart (U): Cancel and re-throw 1 bad dart in current turn. (1 use/leg)
  • 1-5 Keys: Switch Modes | C Key: Campaign Mode

Game Modes

501 / 301: Reach exactly 0. Bust if < 0 or = 1.
Cricket: Close 15-20 and Bullseye (3 hits each).
Around the Clock (ATC): Hit numbers 1 through 20 in sequence.
Bullseye Blitz: First to score 10 Bullseye hits (Outer=1, Inner=2).
