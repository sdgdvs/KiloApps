How to Play KMatch3

Swap adjacent gems to form lines of 3 or more of the same color.

Special Gems:
- Match 4: Line Bomb (destroys row or column).
- Match 5: Rainbow Gem (destroys all gems of selected color).
- T / L Match: 3x3 Bomb Gem (explodes 3x3 area).

Obstacles:
- Ice Tiles: Cannot be moved. Break by matching internal gem.
- Stone Tiles: Locked blocks. Break by matching adjacent gems or using bombs/Hammer!

Game Modes:
- Campaign (15 Stages): Beat target scores in move or timed stages.
- Zen: Infinite relaxing play.
- Timed Rush: Speed challenge against the clock.

Power-ups (500 Score):
- Hammer: Destroy any single gem, ice, or stone tile.
- +Moves / +15s: Add +5 moves or +15 seconds to timer.
- Shuffle: Rearrange all board gems.
