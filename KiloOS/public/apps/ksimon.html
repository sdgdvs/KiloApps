How to Play KSimon

Rules: Observe the pattern of colors/sounds and repeat it back. The sequence grows each round.

Controls:

  • Mouse: Click the colored buttons.
  • Keyboard: Q, W, E (Top Row), A, S, D (Bottom Row) or 1-6 keys.
  • Space: Start game.
  • H: Use Hint to replay sequence.
  • F: Use Slow-Mo for 2x slower replay.
  • J: Shield (absorbs 1 player mistake).

Modes:

  • Classic: Standard 6 color memory game.
  • Reverse: Repeat sequence in reverse order.
  • Speed: Fast flashing playback.
  • Endless: Only the newest color flashes each round.
  • Campaign: 15 stages with varying speeds, target sequence lengths, and Reverse/Chaos modifiers.
  • Chaos: Unpredictable flash speeds and pitch variations!
