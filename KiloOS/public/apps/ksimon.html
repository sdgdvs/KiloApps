How to Play KSimon 3D

Rules: Watch the sequence of LED buttons & tones and repeat it back. The sequence grows each round.

Controls:

  • Mouse: Click the 3D glossy console buttons.
  • Keyboard: Q, W, E (Top Row) / A, S, D (Bottom Row) or 1-6 keys.
  • Space: Start game.
  • H: Hint (Replays current sequence).
  • F: Slow-Mo (2x slower playback speed).
  • J: Shield (Absorbs 1 mistake).

Modes:

  • Classic: Standard 6 color memory game.
  • Reverse: Repeat sequence in reverse order.
  • Speed: Fast flashing playback.
  • Endless: Only the newest color flashes each round.
  • Campaign: 15 stages with varying speeds, target lengths, and modifiers.
  • Chaos: Unpredictable flash speeds and pitch variations!
