How to Play

Rules: Be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of your discs.

Controls: Click any column on the board to drop your disc. Use the buttons below to change modes or undo moves.

Features:

  • 2-Player or vs AI modes
  • Adjustable AI difficulty
  • Stat tracking (Wins, Streaks)
  • Save/Load your game
