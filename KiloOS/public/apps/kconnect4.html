How to Play

Rules: Be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of 4 discs.

Modes: 2 Player, vs AI, 15-Stage Campaign, and Speed Mode.

Power-Ups:

  • Bomb: Clears 3x3 surrounding cells & drops floating discs.
  • Drill: Clears all discs & crackable blocks in the column.
