How to Play KHangman

Rules: Guess the hidden word one letter at a time. You have 6 lives. Incorrect guesses draw parts of the hangman. If the hangman is completed before you guess the word, you lose!

Controls: Click the on-screen keyboard or type using your physical keyboard (A-Z).

Categories: Select from predefined categories (Technology, Animals, Countries, Science).

Custom Words: Select "Custom" to enter your own comma-separated list of words to play with.

Hint: Reveals one correct letter. Can only be used once per game.
