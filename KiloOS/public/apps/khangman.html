How to Play KHangman

Rules: Guess the hidden word one letter at a time using your physical or on-screen keyboard.

Game Modes:

  • Freeplay: Classic un-timed Hangman mode.
  • Campaign Mode: 15 stages of increasing difficulty across categories.
  • Time Attack Blitz: 60s timer! +2s per correct letter, +15s per word solved!

Power-Ups:

  • Bomb: Eliminates 3 incorrect letters from keyboard.
  • Hint: Reveals 1 correct letter in word.
  • Shield: Absorbs 1 wrong letter guess penalty automatically.
