KReversi Strategy Guide & Rules

1. Core Rules & Objective

Objective: Control the majority of discs on the board when no legal moves remain.

Flipping & Sandwiching: Trap opponent discs between your newly placed disc and an existing disc of your color.

Blocked Cells (Holes): Dark unplayable cells that block line flips and cannot hold discs.

2. Campaign Mode & AI Personalities

15 Stages: Progress across dynamic 6x6, 8x8, and 10x10 boards with unique obstacle setups.

4 AI Difficulties: Rookie, Greedy, Positional, and Grandmaster Minimax AI!

3. Power-ups & Shortcuts

Optimal Hint (H): Highlights top positional / minimax move choice.

Undo Move (U): Reverts last move pair against AI.

Bomb Disc (B): Detonates a 3x3 explosion flipping surrounding enemy discs!
