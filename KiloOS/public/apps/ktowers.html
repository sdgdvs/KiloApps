How to Play KTowers

Goal: Move all discs from the first peg to the last peg.

Rules:

  • You can only move one disc at a time.
  • Only the top disc on any peg can be moved.
  • You cannot place a larger disc on top of a smaller disc.

Controls:

  • Click a peg to select the top disc, then click another peg to drop it.
  • Alternatively, use keys 1, 2, 3 to select and move discs.
